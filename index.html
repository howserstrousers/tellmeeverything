As design students at Boston University's School of Visual Arts in 2016 we believe the line between different mediums, forms and practices are all blurring together. So long are the days of the “branding specialist” or “motion designer” Visual culture is exploding in front of our eyes and everyone wants wants a piece of the pie. Tell Me Everything is an art show with a motive to expose young artists as everything they’re worth. 

This is a cross disciplinary art show for one night only. It will showcase emerging artists across all media from Boston, NYC, and LA. This is an opportunity for people who are interested to gain a look into the emerging visual arts scene, and to be a part of something that will not exist after the 3 hour event. In turn, the event will give a quick glimpse into the Student Arts scene and hopefully further exhibiting artists careers.
